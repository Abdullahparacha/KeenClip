About

With the passage of time, the trends change and societies evolve. the Information is available everywhere in the many different forms. Knowledge gaining/sharing has changed in the last decade and many resources are available. One of the most commonly used medium is video, which has become more popular with the increase of the bandwidth of internet. However, due to its inherent nature, a video cannot be used like a text document and this what gave us the idea of KeenClip. Through KeenClip, users can watch and tag videos. Give title and brief description to relevant parts in the videos. This will help them as well as others to avoid watching irrelevant parts in the video, and watch only what is interesting to them.
